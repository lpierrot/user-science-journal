Blogging About Design

Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Lionel Pierrot on May 7, 2019

Purpose and Scope

For this weekâ€™s project, we will be conducting a usability test for Swahili Village website. will be analyzing the user performance on a given task, the navigation menu, website functionality, content and identifying design flows.

I recruited three participants to analyze how well can a user navigate the site menu? How well they understand the content? How well they understand the functionalities on the site? and what thoughts the user has on the overall design? I will also have some specific tasks for them to do and document the results.

Participants

Three friends from different backgrounds graciously accepted to help me do this test. Kid is a program manager for a non-profit organization, Yannick works in customer service, and Sue is a software engineer. They all spend an average of about 9 hours to 12 hours a day browsing the internet for different things. Age ranges: 20 to 35 years.

Kid has visited Swahili Village in the past, so I expect her to be a little familiar with the place and food. But the other two participants have not been there or visited the website before.

Schedule & Location

I reached out to three testers via text message and phone call requesting a one on one meeting at Lemieux Library and McGoldrick Learning Commons, between 9 AM and 12 PM. With the first participant starting at 9 AM, second participant 10 AM and the last participant at 11 PM. Each test lasted about 30 minutes, with at least 15 minutes explaining to them the process, signing consent forms and setting up for the test.

Equipment

WFor equipment, I plan on testing using a MacBook Pro laptop 13 inches, windows desktop, and iPhone 8 Plus to record. I also plan on using different browsers such as Google Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.

Scenarios or tasks

  • Make an online reservation: Can you make an online reservation?
  • Find the menu: Do you understand the menu?
  • Find happy hour menu: What time is happy hour?
  • Cuisine influence: Can you identify the background of this cuisine?
